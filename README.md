# Customer Segmentation Project

## Objective

The objective of this project was to segment customers based on their demographic and spending behavior to identify different customer groups for business insights and targeted marketing.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Mall Customers Dataset

## Dashboard Components

* Total Customers KPI
* Average Age KPI
* Average Annual Income KPI
* Customer Segmentation Analysis (Scatter Chart)
* Customer Segment Distribution (Pie Chart)
* Average Income by Customer Segment (Bar Chart)

## Key Insights

1. Customers were segmented into High Value, Medium Value, and Low Value groups based on spending behavior.
2. High Value customers demonstrated higher spending scores and represent potential premium customers.
3. Customer income and spending patterns varied across segments, providing opportunities for targeted marketing strategies.

## Conclusion

Customer segmentation helps businesses better understand customer behavior and enables data-driven decision-making for improving customer engagement and business growth.
